Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data Collection

  • Personal details: Name, address, PAN number, Aadhaar, phone number.
  • Financial details: Income, credit score, bank account details.
  • Device details: IP address, location, usage behavior.

2. Data Usage

  • To calculate loan eligibility.
  • To share with partner banks/money lenders (only with user consent).
  • For fraud detection and risk management.

3. Data Sharing

  • Sharing data with third-party service providers or partner banks.
  • Sharing limited data with analytics tools for app improvement.

4. Data Security

  • Using encryption and secure data transmission.
  • Implementing strict measures to prevent unauthorized access.

5. User Consent

Data processing is done only after the user agrees to the privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retaining data until the loan process is completed.
  • Deleting data upon user request.

7. User Rights

  • Users can request access to, update, or delete their personal data.
  • Responding promptly to user complaints.

Example Statement: "Our privacy policy clearly explains how your personal data is collected, processed, and shared with partner banks. Your data is safeguarded using encryption techniques to ensure your safety."
